Location

Messi scored a waterfront mansion located in Fort Lauderdale for $10.75 million, according to the listing. The two-story home is located at 91 Compass Lane, Fort Lauderdale, inside the gated community Bay Colony. Messi made the move to the Sunshine State with his family in July to play for Inter Miami CF. The home has eight bedrooms and 10 bathrooms across nearly 10,500 square feet. There's also a spa, fitness room, pool, three-car garage, two docks, and 170 feet of water frontage. The home was originally built in 1988, property records show.
