BORN

Lionel Andrés "Leo" Messi was born on 24 June 1987 in Rosario, Santa Fe Province Argentina. The third of four children of Jorge Messi, a steel factory manager, and his wife Celia Cuccittini, who worked in a magnet manufacturing workshop. On his father's side, he is of Italian and Spanish descent, the great-grandson of immigrants from the north-central Adriatic region of Italy where he developed a passion for the sport from an early age, playing constantly with his older brothers, Rodrigo and Matías, and his cousins.

Education

Lionel Messi went to Las Heras for elementary school. Lionel did not attend college because his potential was identified by FC Barcelona.

Career

Joined the youth team of Newell's Old Boys in 1995, moved to Barcelona at age 13 to receive treatment for a growth hormone deficiency. Made his senior debut for Barcelona at age 17. Became Barcelona's all-time top scorer. Left Barcelona in 2021 to join Paris Saint Germain. Joined Inter Miami in the MLS.

Family

Lionel Messi's wife is Antonela Roccuzzo, and they have three sons: Thiago, Mateo, and Ciro.
